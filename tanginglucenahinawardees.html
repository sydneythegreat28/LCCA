AWARDEES

Objectives of the Search for Natatanging Lucenahin

1.Recognize the important contribution of Outstanding Lucenahin.

2. Inculcate and develop the importance of community service which is essential for fast growing and progressive highly urbanized city.

3. Develop a revitalized ethics for work excellence and personal achievements, and

4. Serve as role models for Residents of the City.1. Recognize the important contribution of Outstanding Lucenahin.

2. Inculcate and develop the importance of community service which is essential for fast growing and progressive highly urbanized city.

3. Develop a revitalized ethics for work excellence and personal achievements, and

4. Serve as role models for Residents of the City.



GUIDELINES AND CRITERIA

1. A natural born Lucenahin;

2. If not born in Lucena City, must have resided in Lucena City for at least fifteen (15) years and/ or must have been a recipient of a resolution adopting him/her as a distinguished son/daughter of Lucena City.

3. Has rendered significant and meritorious service in the City of Lucena and the country as a whole which brought honor to the city;

4. Selection shall be along the categories of, if any;

a.Government/Public Service
b. Community Service
c. Arts
a. Performing Arts
b. Arts & Letter
d. Sports
e. Education
f. Business Entrepreneurs, Trade and Industry
g. Agriculture
h. Science and Technology
i. Public Health
j. Religion
k. Professional

5.Posthumous award is given to deceased but deserving Lucenahin who met the criteria.
